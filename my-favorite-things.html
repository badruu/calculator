KARIS

classic karis

karis can be defined as the the product obtained after dying fabric

karis is considered as part of tradition by some communities. sometimes, it is worn for special occassions such as weddings and baby showers.

From west Africa to East Africa, a lot of communities do karis even though the style and art might be different.

Listed below are the origin of the karis styles that have fascinated me the most

  1. West Africa especially Mali and Mauritania
  2. India
  3. Indonesia
  4. Somalia especially Hargeysa and Mogadishu
